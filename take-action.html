You Are Stronger Than You Think

If you want to see a change, you have to be the change. Do not expect someone else to help get you there, get there yourself. Refuse to be the victim they want you to be, you are a powerful woman, be confident in your abilities and if you do not like what you see, change it.

  • In 2018, an estimated 734,630 people said that they were sexually harassed.
  • Sixty six percent of adolescent victims did not tell a parent or any other adult about the assault.
  • Only 19 percent of victims reported the assault to the police.
